⚡ Strategic Reality Check
RiskRunway owns Phases 1–3: submission organization, appetite routing, pipeline velocity, speed to bind. It's a broad platform play across retail, wholesale, and carrier layers.

PFConverge owns Phase 4: quote data extraction and finance agreement generation inside AMS360. It's narrow, deep, and solves a specific daily pain for PF teams — but only activates after a policy binds and only if the client finances.

The overlap zone (quote aggregation / comparison) is the battleground. RiskRunway wants to track quotes as pipeline data. PFConverge wants to extract and normalize quote data for finance. These are compatible if positioned right — RiskRunway is the pipeline, PFConverge is the finance handoff layer.
